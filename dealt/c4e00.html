Meanings

  • One. The number one, the first. [source(s): Shogakukan]
  • A unit. [source(s): Shogakukan]
  • To become one; to be the same. To unify, to unite. [同] [source(s): Shogakukan]
  • Unity, union, uniformity. [source(s): Shogakukan]
  • A, an, the; once. [source(s): Shogakukan]
  • Whole, all, throughout. [全] [source(s): Shogakukan]
  • For once, for a first time, as a first attempt. [source(s): Shogakukan]
  • Each, per; every time. [source(s): Shogakukan]
  • All of a sudden. [亟] [source(s): Shogakukan]
  • Variant of 弌. [source(s): Shogakukan]
